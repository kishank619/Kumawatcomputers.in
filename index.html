Chiranjeevi Swasthya Bima Yojana
Health

Free medical insurance up to ₹25 lakh per year for Rajasthan families. Government pays premium for BPL/NFSA.

Required Documents
  • Jan Aadhar Card
  • Aadhar Card (all family members)
  • Mobile number linked with Jan Aadhar
  • Ration Card (if BPL / NFSA)
  • Bank Passbook / Account details
🆓 Free for BPL | ₹850/yr others आवेदन करें
👨‍👩‍👧
Palanhar Yojana
Social Welfare

Monthly financial aid for orphan or abandoned children raised by relatives. ₹500 (0–6yr) and ₹1000 (6–18yr) per month.

Required Documents
  • Child's Birth Certificate
  • Aadhar Card of child & guardian
  • Jan Aadhar Card
  • Parents' Death Certificate (if applicable)
  • Talak / Court order (if applicable)
  • Income Certificate (Below ₹1.20 lakh/yr)
  • Bank Passbook (guardian's)
🆓 Free Scheme आवेदन करें

Official income proof issued by Rajasthan Government, required for scholarships, schemes, and legal purposes.

Required Documents
  • Aadhar Card
  • Jan Aadhar Card
  • Ration Card
  • Self-declaration of income (affidavit optional)
  • Bhamashah Card (if old)

Identity

Proof of permanent residence in Rajasthan. Required for government jobs, admissions, and various schemes.

Required Documents
  • Aadhar Card
  • Jan Aadhar Card
  • Ration Card

Old Age Pension (वृद्धावस्था पेंशन)
Social Welfare

Monthly pension for senior citizens above 55 (women) and 58 (men) years. ₹750–₹1000 per month.

Required Documents
  • Aadhar Card
  • Jan Aadhar Card
  • Age Proof (Birth Certificate / Voter ID / School TC)
  • Income Certificate (₹48,000 limit/yr)
  • Bank Passbook
  • Passport Photo (2 copies)

NSP / Rajasthan Scholarship
Education

National & state scholarships for SC/ST/OBC/EWS students. Pre-matric and Post-matric schemes.

Required Documents
  • Aadhar Card (student)
  • Jan Aadhar Card
  • Caste Certificate
  • Income Certificate
  • Last year's marksheet
  • Bonafide / School enrollment certificate
  • Bank Passbook (student's)
